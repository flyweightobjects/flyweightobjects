Persist<(Of <(T>)>)(FlyweightSet<(Of <(T>)>))
Performs all necessary operations on the supplied IFlyweightSet object. Note that all objects which have been removed from the IFlyweightSet will be deleted, while the others will either be inserted or deleted based upon their status in storage.
